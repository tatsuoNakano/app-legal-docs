Tatsuo Nakano Privacy Policy

Effective: September 15, 2025  |  Last Updated: September 15, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. It is Tatsuo Nakano's policy to respect your privacy and comply with any applicable law and regulation regarding any personal information we may collect about you, including via our app (“This App”) and its associated services.

Personal information is any information about you which can be used to identify you. This includes information about you as a person (such as name, address, and date of birth), your devices, payment details, and information about how you use an app or online service.

If our app contains links to third-party sites and services, please be aware those sites and services have their own privacy policies. After following a link to any third-party content, you should read their posted privacy policy. This Privacy Policy does not apply to your activities after you leave our app.

Information We Collect

Information we collect falls into one of two categories: voluntarily provided and automatically collected.

Voluntarily Provided

Any information you knowingly and actively provide us when using our app and services.

Automatically Collected

Information automatically sent by your device in the course of accessing our app and services.

Log Data

When you access our servers via our app, we may automatically log standard data provided by your device. This may include your IP address, device type and OS version, app version, language, time zone, your activity within the app, and time/date stamps. When errors occur, we may collect crash and performance data (e.g., stack traces, device state, network status).

Personal Information

  • Email address
  • In-app purchase status (from Google Play / Apple App Store, no card numbers)
  • Advertising IDs (Google AAID, Apple IDFA) – used for serving personalized and non-personalized ads through Google AdMob
  • Analytics identifiers (Firebase Installation ID)
  • Usage metrics (sessions, screens viewed, features used)
  • Approximate location (region-level, from IP)

Third-Party SDKs and Services

  • Google AdMob / AdSense – ads and monetization (uses Advertising IDs for personalized and non-personalized ads)
  • Google Firebase Analytics – usage analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics – crash reporting
  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchases – payment processing

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such information is discovered, we will delete it promptly.

Security & Data Retention

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your data. Retention lasts only as long as necessary for service provision and legal obligations. Afterwards, data is deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights & Controls

  • Opt-out of personalized ads (via device settings on iOS/Android)
  • Control analytics tracking (via device or in-app settings)
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information

Disclosure of Personal Information

We may disclose personal information to service providers, employees, regulators, or in connection with business transfers. We do not sell personal information for money. Some U.S. state laws consider ad/analytics sharing a “sale” or “sharing,” and you may have the right to opt out.

Changes to This Policy
